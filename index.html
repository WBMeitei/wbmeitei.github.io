I am pursuing a full-time PhD in Biostatistics and Demography at the International Institute for Population Sciences (IIPS), Mumbai in the Department of Public Health and Mortality Studies, IIPS, Mumbai under the supervision of Prof. Abhishek Singh. My PhD work focuses on modelling and identifying the risk factors associated with mortality among children at early ages in India. My PhD thesis extensively examines the effect of early childhood mortality risk factors, such as postnatal care, umbilical cord care, undernutrition, the age pattern of mortality, and the impact of community clustering. I have submitted my PhD Thesis, and I'm awaiting my final thesis defence after receiving the examiner's comments and suggestions

My research interest includes Probability Theory in Statistics, Modelling the transition between co-morbid diseases, Spatial Epidemiology and identifying the risk factors or determinants of Maternal & Child Health and Childhood Mortality.

I graduated from the D. M. College of Science, Imphal, with Statistics (honours) in 2014. Later I completed the Post Graduate Diploma in Statistical Methods and Analytics (PGDSMA) from Indian Statistical Institute (ISI), Tezpur (2014-15 batch). Then in 2015, I joined IIPS, Mumbai for an MSc degree in Biostatistics and Demography (2015-17 batch). I continue for MPhil degree in the same institute (2018-19 batch). I was awarded the “Prof. Tara Kanitkar Gold Medal” for securing the first rank in MPhil Biostatistics & Demography. Before I joined the MPhil program, I had also worked as a "Research Assistant" at IIPS, Mumbai under the project entitled “Goal Setting and Identification of Priority Interventions for Odisha using Lives Saved Tool (LiST): An Analysis Based on NFHS-4 Data”.

I would like to see myself as a data scientist analysing data to answer research questions on diverse real-world problems. Throughout my academic journey, I have stressed myself in developing excellent technical writing and analytical skills using various statistical software. I have published several articles and attended several conferences and workshops presenting papers on research areas, viz., undernutrition, diabetes, maternal and child health, etc. Thus, I have expertise in handling large-scale data using statistical software such as R, STATA, SPSS, Base SAS, Excel etc, including data mining, data cleaning, ensuring data quality and creating reports with clear visualisation.

Also, I am an R enthusiast. And I love coding in R.
