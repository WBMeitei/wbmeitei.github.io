About

I'm a Junior Research Scientist in GEH-Project at International Institute for Population Sciences, Mumbai.

I have submitted my PhD Thesis in Biostatistics and Demography for the external examiner’s review at International Institute for Population Sciences, Mumbai, under the supervision of Prof. Abhishek Singh, and awaiting the examiner's comments for my final thesis defence to be scheduled.

My research primarily focuses on the empirical analysis of population health and well-being using standard and advanced statistical and epidemiological methods. More specifically, my research interest includes studying the dynamics of gender inequality due to household/family hierarchy, community effects on health, transition-specific risk factors of co-morbid diseases, and trends and risk factors of early childhood mortality, adult mortality, and undernutrition. I use R software and STATA predominantly (but not limited to) for reproducible reporting of my research findings. I am also interested in educational activities involving R.

Before joining the PhD programme, I graduated from the D. M. College of Science, Imphal, with Statistics (honours) in 2014. Later, I completed the Post Graduate Diploma in Statistical Methods and Analytics (PGDSMA) from Indian Statistical Institute, Tezpur (2014-15 cohort). Then, in 2015, I joined International Institute for Population Sciences, Mumbai, for an MSc degree in Biostatistics and Demography (2015-17 cohort). I continued my MPhil degree in the same institute (2018-19 cohort). I was awarded the “Prof. Tara Kanitkar Gold Medal” for securing the first rank in MPhil Biostatistics & Demography.

I had also worked as a research assistant at International Institute for Population Sciences, Mumbai in the project “Goal Setting and Identification of Priority Interventions for Odisha using Lives Saved Tool (LiST): An Analysis Based on NFHS-4 (2015-16)”.

I would like to see myself as a proficient scholar, analysing data to answer research questions on diverse real-world problems. Throughout my academic journey, I have stressed myself in developing excellent technical writing and analytical skills using various statistical software. I have published several articles and attended conferences and workshops presenting papers on research areas, viz., undernutrition, diabetes, maternal & child health, early childhood mortality etc. I also have skilful knowledge in handling large-scale data using statistical software such as R, STATA, SPSS, MS Excel, ArcGIS, GeoDa etc., including data mining, data cleaning, ensuring data quality and creating reports with clear visualisation.
