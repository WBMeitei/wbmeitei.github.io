The MSc and MPhil dissertation, under the supervision of Prof. Laishram Ladusing at International Institute for Population Sciences (IIPS), focused on modelling the transition between co-morbid diseases (such as Diabetes & Hypertension and the transition of maternal morbidities during a reproductive cycle), Spatial Epidemiology and identifying the risk factors or determinants of Maternal Health. And while working as a Research Assistant in the project at IIPS, I focused on modelling the level of stunting and wasting in the state of Odisha, India from 2015 to 2030 by scaling up maternal and child health interventions under alternative scenarios. Currently I am working on my PhD Thesis related to "U5 mortality in India using Conventional as well as Bayesian statistical techniques" under the supervision of Prof. Abhishek Singh at the Department of Public Health and Mortality Studies, IIPS.

I've also contributed in the studies of Global Burden of Disease & Local Burden of Disease. Here are the studies that I've contributed.
